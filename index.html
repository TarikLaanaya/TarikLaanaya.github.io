Case Study – Forest Quest

Goal

Create a short narrative experience that teaches mechanics through exploration.

My Responsibilities

  • Core gameplay loop design
  • Level pacing & player onboarding
  • Iteration based on playtesting

Design Challenges

Players initially missed key paths. I solved this by adjusting lighting and landmarks.

What I Learned

Environmental cues are more effective than explicit UI.
